5 simple examples to understand Elasticsearch aggregation

Elasticsearch aggregation give us the ability to ask questions to our data. The ability to group and find out statistics (such as sum, average, min, max) on our data by using a simple search query.

In this post, we will see some very simple examples to understand how powerful and easy it is to use Elasticsearch aggregation. I will also share a postman collection link at the bottom of this post in case you want to try out these queries on your own.
